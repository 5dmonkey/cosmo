Tan-France-inspired direction

Ben in 10 outfits

The brief: masculine, warm, welcoming, grounded, cool. Less gimmick, less overdone bomber, no flowery flat-brim. More texture, better jackets, stronger trousers, and a quieter kind of sex appeal.

Look 10
Glasses upgrade

The Better Orange Glasses

Keep the warmth, refine the shape. He wants smarter lines.

• softer amber or tobacco acetate
• slightly slimmer frame
• less novelty, more intention
• think architectural, not quirky
Tan’s blunt summary: Ben doesn’t need louder clothes — he needs better clothes. Better fabric, better jacket shapes, cleaner hats, richer neutrals, and fewer pieces that wear him instead of the other way around.
