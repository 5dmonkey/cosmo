Sample Montecito / Santa Barbara Wedding Budget Ranges

A general planning reference based on comparable Santa Barbara and Montecito events. Actual totals vary depending on guest count, private home logistics, vendor level, design scope, rentals, parking/transportation, and service expectations.

This is intended as a general guide only. It does not reflect any one private client budget and has been anonymized to protect prior client confidentiality.
Category Typical Range General Notes
Planning / Coordination $12,000–$25,000+ Varies by level of planning support, design involvement, production complexity, and event weekend scope.
Catering + Bar $20,000–$45,000+ Private home events often require more staffing, kitchen support, rentals, bar setup, and service labor than venue-based weddings.
Rentals $8,000–$20,000+ Tables, chairs, linens, tabletop, bars, lounge, kitchen equipment, tenting, heaters, and installation can add up quickly. Comparable past rental examples have exceeded $11,000 before additional fees.
Florals $6,000–$20,000+ Driven by ceremony design, table count, flower choice, installation work, repurposing, and labor.
Photography $5,000–$15,000+ Coverage hours, second shooter, film, rehearsal coverage, and deliverables all affect pricing.
Music / Entertainment $4,000–$15,000+ Ranges vary widely between a DJ, live ceremony musicians, and full band production.
Lighting / Production $5,000–$15,000+ Often needed at private homes for ambiance, safety, band support, dining areas, pathways, restrooms, and power. Comparable past production examples have reached about $9,000 for lighting alone.
Hair + Makeup $1,500–$5,000+ Depends on bridal services, trial(s), number of additional people, touch-up time, and travel.
Transportation / Valet / Parking $2,000–$8,000+ Especially relevant for private homes in Montecito where parking, guest flow, neighborhood considerations, and shuttling may need to be managed. Comparable valet examples have been around $3,000.
Cake / Desserts $800–$3,500+ Final cost depends on cake size, design, delivery, setup, and whether additional desserts are included.
Staffing / Additional Event Support $1,500–$6,000+ May include event assistants, production support, setup crews, hospitality support, or added coordination staffing depending on scope.
Taxes / Delivery / Service Fees / Gratuities 8%–25%+ One of the most commonly underestimated parts of the budget. These charges can materially change the final total.
Contingency 5%–10% Helpful to reserve for late additions, weather pivots, final rentals, or production adjustments.

Prepared as a general educational reference for budget planning purposes. Final budgets are always shaped by actual guest count, venue conditions, rentals, service style, and aesthetic direction.
