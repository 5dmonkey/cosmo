1st Thursday Art Walk

An evening of open galleries, cultural spaces, art, music, and downtown wandering.

WhenFirst Thursday of each month
WhereDowntown Santa Barbara

1st Thursday Art Walk is one of the clearest examples of Santa Barbara’s culture feeling active, walkable, and social without becoming chaotic. Galleries, creative spaces, and local businesses open their doors for an easy night of discovery.

This is the kind of event that helps the app feel curated rather than merely informational — art, atmosphere, and local rhythm in one place.
