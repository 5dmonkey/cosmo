Santa Barbara Farmers Market

Fresh produce, flowers, pantry finds, and local rhythm across Santa Barbara County.

WhenMultiple weekly market days
WhereDowntown SB, Old Town, Montecito + more

The Santa Barbara Certified Farmers Market Association has been connecting local growers and the community since 1983. It’s one of the easiest ways to feel the living culture of Santa Barbara: seasonal fruit, flowers, prepared food, and a distinctly local pace.

Whether you’re planning your week or simply seeing what the city is offering, this is one of the easiest ways to feel connected to Santa Barbara’s everyday rhythm.
