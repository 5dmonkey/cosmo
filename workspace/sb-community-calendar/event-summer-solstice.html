Summer Solstice Celebration

One of Santa Barbara’s most iconic annual festival weekends.

WhenFestival weekend · June 19–21
WhereSanta Barbara

The Summer Solstice Celebration is part parade, part creative release, part collective seasonal mood. It’s one of those events that belongs at the center of a Santa Barbara calendar because it captures local identity, energy, and spectacle.

It’s the kind of annual celebration people plan around — vibrant, communal, and unmistakably Santa Barbara.
